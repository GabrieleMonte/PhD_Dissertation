\begin{vita}

Gabriele Montefalcone was born and raised in Italy. He received his undergraduate degree from University College London and his master's degree from the University of Cambridge. He then joined the Weinberg Institute for Theoretical Physics at the University of Texas at Austin, where he pursued his doctoral research in theoretical cosmology under the supervision of Katherine Freese.

\end{vita}
